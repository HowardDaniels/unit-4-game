Crystals Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. If your score goes over the random number, you lose the game.

The crystals' values are hidden until you click on them.

Each time the game starts, the crystals are reassigned new values.
